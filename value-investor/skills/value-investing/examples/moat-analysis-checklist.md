## Section 2: Moat Strength Assessment

**Total Moat Score:** _____ / 15

- 0-3: No moat → Pass on investment
- 4-6: Weak moat → Require large margin of safety (40%+)
- 7-9: Moderate moat → Good candidate with adequate margin (25-40%)
- 10-12: Wide moat → Can pay fair prices (25% margin)
- 13-15: Exceptional moat → Rare, premium justified

**Number of moat types present:** _____

- 0: No moat (pass)
- 1: Single moat (fragile)
- 2+: Multiple moats (reinforcing advantages)
