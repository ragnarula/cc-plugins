**Shareholder Letters:**
- [ ] Detailed, thoughtful, honest about mistakes

- [ ] Minimal or no letters

**Earnings Calls:**
- [ ] Direct answers, addresses tough questions
- [ ] Generally responsive
- [ ] Evasive on difficult topics
- [ ] Promotional, avoids substance

**Discussion of Mistakes:**

## Buffett's Management Checklist

- [ ] "Think like owners" - Long-term value focus over short-term metrics
- [ ] Candid communication - Honest about mistakes and challenges
- [ ] Rational decision-making - Disciplined capital allocation
- [ ] Resist institutional imperative - Don't follow peer behavior
- [ ] Appropriate compensation - Aligned with long-term shareholders

**How many boxes checked?**
- 5/5: Exceptional
- 4/5: Good
- 3/5: Adequate
- <3/5: Concerning
